A small framed series of truisms hand-set in display caps, written specifically for the apartment you have rented for the week. The truisms address the kitchen, the bathroom, the upstairs neighbor, the noise the heater makes. They are real, and they are hung in real Airbnbs. The host gets a discount on their cleaning service in exchange.
