42 and counting. Each one starts as a doodle on a hotel notepad or the back of an envelope, gets a title later, and ends up scanned. The titles are how the world enters: "the moon eels are leaving," "computers are made of rocks," "may your aim be cosmic and true." Sometimes I think the titles are the project and the drawings are the supporting material.
