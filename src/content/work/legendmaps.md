I started Legend Maps because I wanted a piece of software that felt like a kitchen table, not a kiosk. Two people sit down with a fake map and start making things up. The product is a thin shell around that conversation. We launched in early 2023, picked up a small loyal audience of D&D groups and elementary-school teachers, and have been quietly iterating ever since.
